Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

1. Introduction

Qaher Logistics ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, company name
  • Shipment Details: Origin, destination, weight, cargo type
  • Account Information: Username, password, account preferences
  • Cookies: Automatically collected browsing data
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, clicks, referral source

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Process and fulfill shipping requests
  • Provide real-time tracking and notifications
  • Send quotes and pricing information
  • Respond to customer inquiries and support requests
  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party logistics partners, payment processors
  • Legal Requirements: Law enforcement when required by law
  • Business Partners: Only with your explicit consent

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to improve your experience. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Blocking cookies may affect site functionality.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption, firewalls, and secure servers to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide services or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request a copy of your data

10. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Continued use of our site constitutes acceptance of changes. We will notify you of significant changes via email or website notice.
