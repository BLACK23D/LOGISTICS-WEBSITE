Tracking & Delivery

You can track your shipment on our Tracking Page. Simply enter your tracking number (e.g., QHL-2024-12345) to get real-time updates on your cargo location, estimated delivery time, and current status. You'll also receive email notifications at key milestones.

While 95% of our shipments arrive on time, delays can occur due to unforeseen circumstances. We recommend contacting our Support Team immediately. Our 24/7 team will investigate and provide updates. If delay is due to our negligence, we offer compensation as per our terms.

Address changes may be possible before your shipment reaches the transit hub. Contact our team immediately with your tracking number. Depending on shipment status, we may charge a small fee. Changes cannot be made once delivery has commenced.

Pricing & Quotes

Our rates are based on: (1) Service type (Air, Sea, Land), (2) Weight and dimensions, (3) Origin and destination, (4) Handling requirements (Temperature-controlled, Hazmat, etc.), (5) Speed preference (Express vs Economy). Use our Quote Calculator for instant estimates.

Yes! We offer volume discounts for businesses shipping regularly. Enterprise accounts receive 10-25% discounts depending on monthly shipment volume. Contact our sales team to discuss custom pricing packages.

No hidden fees! Our quotes include all standard charges: transport, handling, insurance (optional), and applicable taxes. Any special services (weekend delivery, express notifications) are clearly itemized before confirmation.

Services & Shipments

We handle most cargo types: electronics, textiles, machinery, perishables, and more. Restricted items include: hazardous materials (unless properly licensed), firearms, counterfeit goods, and illegal substances. Contact us to verify if your cargo type can be shipped.

Yes, cargo insurance is optional. We provide coverage up to the declared value. Insurance typically costs 1-2% of shipment value. It covers loss, damage, and theft during transit. Basic coverage ($5,000) is free; higher amounts require additional premium.
